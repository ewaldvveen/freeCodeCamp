Living on the moon? No problem.

Are you in the situation that you're not sure if you should move to the moon or not, because you don't know how you can buy new shoes from there? Relax! We've got your back. At the moment, from every pair of shoes we sell at the shop, we save 80% of the income to buy our own rocket in the future. With this rocket we can deliver you your shoes without any problems.
